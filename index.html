Il Caffè Buono

"Senso Unico" means "one way" in Italian. The name reflects Mario's desire to recreate the kind of ambiance - and il cafeè buono - he experienced in his native city of Piacenza, in northern Italy. To his mind, the one way to enjoy that kind of experience here in Seattle is to walk through Caffè Senso Unico's door.
